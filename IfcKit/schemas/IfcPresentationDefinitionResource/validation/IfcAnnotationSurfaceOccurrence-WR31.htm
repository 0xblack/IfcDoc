The Item that is styled by an IfcAnnotationSurfaceOccurrence relation shall be (if provided) a subtype of IfcSurface, IfcSolidModel, IfcShellBasedSurfaceModel, IfcFaceBasedSurfaceModel.
NOTE  If no Item is provided, the IfcAnnotationSurfaceOccurrence is used to provide a surface style to a material dependent presentation.
